Frequently Asked Questions

To cancel your reservation, log in to your account, go to "My Bookings," select the reservation you want to cancel, and click the "Cancel" button. The refund amount will depend on the hotel's cancellation policy, which is displayed on your booking confirmation. Some bookings may be non-refundable as indicated at the time of booking.


You can modify your reservation by logging in, going to "My Bookings," selecting the reservation, and clicking "Modify." If the new dates have different rates, you may need to pay the difference or receive a partial refund. Some reservations may not be modifiable - in this case, you'll need to cancel (if possible) and make a new booking.


For past stays, you can find your receipt in your account under "Booking History." Select the completed stay and click "View Receipt" to download a PDF copy. If you need a receipt for a recent stay that isn't showing up, or if you need a specialized invoice, please contact our customer support team.


Our Best Price Guarantee ensures you get the lowest price for your hotel booking. If you find a lower rate for the same hotel, room type, and dates on another website within 24 hours of making your reservation, we'll match that price and give you an additional 10% discount on your booking.


To redeem Serenity Rewards points, log in to your account, search for a hotel as usual, and on the payment page, you'll see an option to "Use Points" if you have enough points for your stay. You can choose to pay entirely with points or use a points + cash combination for eligible bookings.
